Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our hospital and services.

Our OPD hours are Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7. Patient visiting hours for admitted patients are 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM and 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM.
Yes, we accept cashless insurance from all major providers including Star Health, ICICI Lombard, HDFC Ergo, Bajaj Allianz, and New India Assurance. Please carry your insurance card and ID when visiting.
You can book an appointment through our website (scroll up to the booking section), call us at +91-22-12345678, or send us a WhatsApp message. You can also walk in to the hospital during OPD hours.
